Migration and Income Trends in Australian States

Western Australia has emerged as a significant destination for migrants, particularly those who have lived in the country for over ten years. This trend highlights the long-term integration of individuals from diverse backgrounds into the region, contributing to its cultural and economic landscape. Notably, a substantial majority of overseas-born residents in Western Australia fall into this category, indicating a strong historical connection to the state. The unemployment rate for people born overseas across Australia stands at 5.6%, which is notably higher than the national average of 4.2%.
