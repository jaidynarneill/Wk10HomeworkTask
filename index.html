Immigration in Australia

This visualization presents the immigration patterns in Australia, focusing on the proportion of people born overseas. The data highlights how different regions vary in the number of people from other countries, giving insights into Australia's diverse cultural landscape. The choropleth map above visually represents this data.

By examining the regions with the highest concentrations of foreign-born individuals, we can gain a better understanding of Australia's role as a destination for immigrants and how this impacts its societal structure.
